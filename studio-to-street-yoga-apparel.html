studio to street

Yoga apparel for the part after class.

Studio-to-street usually means polished activewear. Hot yoga asks for something more specific: a layer that understands sweat.

After class is its own category.

The body is still warm. The clothes are not fully dry. You may be going home, to coffee, or into the rest of your day.

Soft matters.

After heat, the wrong fabric can feel loud on skin. A good layer should feel calm, breathable, and easy to keep on.

Coverage should not feel like hiding.

The best studio-to-street pieces make the transition feel intentional. They cover without looking improvised.

yogacloak is for that transition.

The Cloak and The Wrap are designed for post-hot-yoga movement: studio door, sidewalk, car, home.
