One

on the brand

What is yogacloak?

yogacloak is the layer for after class — not during. Hot yoga leaves you damp, flushed, and underdressed for whatever comes next. The Cloak and the Wrap are built for that exact in-between: from the studio doorway to your car, your coffee, your own front door.

Soft, absorbent, dark, and quiet. Made to be pulled on still warm.

Who's behind it?

A small team based in Scottsdale, Arizona — building slowly, in small drops, with intention. Every piece is sampled, worn, washed, worn again before it ships.

When does it launch?

The first drop ships November 2027. Reservations open now to lock in your spot — only 100 of each piece in the first run.

Two

on reserving

How does the reservation work?

A small deposit holds your spot in the first drop. When we open for the full launch, your deposit gets applied toward the final price — you only pay the remainder.

The Cloak — $20 deposit, $98 final.
The Wrap — $15 deposit, $68 final.
Both — $35 deposit, $166 final.

Is the deposit refundable?

The deposit is non-refundable but fully transferable — you can pass your spot to a friend at any point before we ship.

If we end up unable to fulfill your reservation for any reason on our end, you'll get a full refund automatically.

When will I be charged the rest?

About 30 days before shipping. You'll get an email with the final amount, your size selection confirmation, and shipping details. No surprises.

Can I change or cancel my reservation?

You can change your size, shipping address, or transfer your reservation to someone else any time before the final-balance email. Just email us at hello@yogacloak.com.

Because the deposit is non-refundable, we can't cancel and refund — but we can always help you transfer it.

Three

on the cloth

What's it made of?

Brushed recycled microfiber. The Cloak runs around 300 GSM — heavier, more structured, deeply absorbent. The Wrap is closer to 200 GSM — lighter, packable, the one that throws into a tote.

Both are matte graphite, designed to look quiet against everything you already wear.

What sizes does The Cloak come in?

Two sizes, both unisex and intentionally oversized.

S/M fits XS–M, roughly 5'2" to 5'8".
L/XL fits L–XL, roughly 5'7" to 6'2".

If you're between sizes, size down — it's already cut generously.

Does the Wrap come in sizes?

The Wrap is one size. A generously cut rectangular drape — you knot it, sling it, or throw it over a shoulder. Works on anyone.

How do I care for it?

Machine wash cold with like colors. Tumble dry low or hang dry. No fabric softener — it coats the fibers and kills the absorbency.

The fabric gets softer with every wash. Built to last.
