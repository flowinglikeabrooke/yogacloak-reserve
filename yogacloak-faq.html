The pieces & care
The Cloak is the fuller layer: hooded and open-front, falling to mid-thigh with two hidden snaps, around 300 GSM. For the drive home. The Wrap is lighter — an oversized rectangular drape with a single snap, around 200 GSM, packs down to nothing. For the walk home.
Brushed recycled microfiber in matte graphite — deeply absorbent, soft the second it touches skin, and dark enough to go anywhere. Roughly 300 GSM for the Cloak, 200 for the Wrap.
Both are one size, cut oversized to drape rather than fit. They're meant to fall the same whether you're coming off a hot class or a slow cool-down.
Machine wash cold, hang or lay flat to dry. Skip the softener — the fabric is soft on its own and stays more absorbent without it.
